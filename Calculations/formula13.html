Power of a Product Rule

(ab)m = ambm

Formula Explanation

When raising a product to a power, you can distribute the exponent to each factor. This fundamental rule of exponents states that (ab)m = ambm. For example, (2 × 3)2 = 22 × 32 = 4 × 9 = 36.
